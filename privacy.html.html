2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • Location data (GPS, IP address, city)
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, advertising ID)
  • Usage analytics or behavioural tracking
  • Crash reports sent to external servers
  • Any Vastu project data you create

3. Data Storage — Your Device Only

All data you enter in Samartha Vastu is stored exclusively on your own device using browser localStorage. This includes:

  • Client names, places, and phone numbers you enter
  • Site dimensions and room layouts
  • Saved projects (up to 20)
  • App preferences (language, grid settings)

This data never leaves your device. We have no access to it. If you uninstall the app or clear browser data, all stored data is permanently deleted.

4. Sharing Features

Samartha Vastu includes optional sharing features:

  • WhatsApp Share: When you click the WhatsApp button, a project summary is composed on your device and opened in WhatsApp. We do not receive this data — it goes directly from your device to WhatsApp.
  • Email Share: When you click Email, your device's email app opens with a pre-filled summary. We do not receive this data.
  • Export .json: Downloads a project file to your device. We do not receive this file.
  • Floor Plan Image: Downloads a PNG to your device. We do not receive this image.

5. Third-Party Services

The app loads the following third-party resources when you have an internet connection:

  • Font Awesome (cdnjs.cloudflare.com): Icon fonts. Subject to Cloudflare's privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com): Decorative fonts for the splash screen. Subject to Google's privacy policy. The app works fully offline without these fonts.
  • Magnetic Declination tool: An optional external link to magnetic-declination.com for True North calculation. Only opened if you click it.

All other app functionality works completely offline with no network calls.

6. Permissions

Samartha Vastu requests only the following device permissions:

  • Storage / Local Storage: To save your projects on your device.
  • Internet access: Only to load Font Awesome icons and optional Google Fonts. All core Vastu calculations work offline.

We do not request camera, microphone, location, contacts, or any other sensitive permissions.

7. Children's Privacy

Samartha Vastu is a professional tool intended for architects and adults. It is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Security

Since no personal data is transmitted or stored on any server, there is no risk of a data breach from our side. Your project data is as secure as your own device.

9. Changes to This Policy
